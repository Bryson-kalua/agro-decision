About The Project

The Malawi Agro Decision Advisor System (ADAS) is designed to empower farmers with intelligent tools for crop recommendation, climate analysis, irrigation advice, and profit estimation.

The system supports sustainable agriculture and aligns with Malawi 2063 goals by promoting agricultural productivity, commercialization, and technology-driven farming solutions.
